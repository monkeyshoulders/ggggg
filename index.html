About Me

I'm Matt and I have done a lot of things. Currently I work as a UX designer and web developer for Ritz Marketing. I also design sound for commercials and podcasts, as well as teach/train the staff on how to best leverage tech and business software.

I’ve done a lot of things since being a theatre major from bartending to construction to prison guard. I wasn’t happy in any of these jobs, so when my kids were born I decided to stay home and teach myself web development. I had a lot of fun doing this but I found I was more concerned how and why the app or page functioned this way and designing something other than canned websites. I found UX through a local meetup group and I was hooked.

This is what I focus on during the week, solving design problems and then developing them. On the weekends I play music, travel, and hang out with my wife and kids. My journey is far from over but for the first time in a long time I am thoroughly enjoying it.
